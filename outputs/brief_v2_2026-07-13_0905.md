CPIAUCSL (May 2026, 73 days stale) is noted as background; June data due July 14 is flagged as a critical watch item. INDPRO (May 2026, 73 days stale) is used for background context only and does not appear in calculations.

**OPEC basket price:** The OPEC scraper returned null data. No reliable web override was found. The OPEC basket price is excluded from all calculations; Brent is used as the primary oil price reference throughout.

**UAE fiscal breakeven:** $55/bbl is the IMF 2026 estimate for UAE. This is a structural estimate and may differ from the actual Abu Dhabi fiscal breakeven (~$50/bbl) or the consolidated UAE figure. Used for directional fiscal surplus calculation only.

**Petrochemical feedstock base:** AED 17.5bn/yr is an industry estimate for UAE petrochemical sector feedstock costs, sourced from ADNOC/industry reports in state.json context. The 60% pass-through rate is the standard industry assumption for naphtha/ethane feedstock pricing relative to crude.

**Operation 300bn pace:** The AED 13.4bn/yr historical pace is calculated as AED 67bn (200−133) divided by 5 years (2021–2026). The required pace of AED 20.0bn/yr is calculated as AED 100bn remaining divided by 5.0 years to 2031 from mid-2026. The gap-closed percentage uses the correct method: (current − baseline) / (target − baseline) = (200−133)/(300−133) = 40.1%, not 200/300 = 66.7%.

**AED/USD peg:** Fixed at 3.6725 AED/USD. All USD-to-AED conversions use this rate.
